# 📦📊 Customer Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer orders, product performance, sales, profitability, and shipping operations. This project transforms raw supply chain data into actionable business insights using Power BI, Power Query, and DAX.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The Customer Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard helps businesses monitor key supply chain metrics and make informed decisions. The dashboard is divided into five interactive pages covering executive insights, product performance, customer behavior, shipping efficiency, and profitability.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Understand customer purchasing patterns.
* Monitor shipping performance and delivery delays.
* Track order status and profitability.
* Create an interactive business intelligence dashboard.

# 📂 Dashboard Pages

## 📊 Page 1 – Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### KPIs

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📈 Total Profit
* 📦 Total Orders
* 👥 Total Customers
* 🛒 Total Quantity Sold
* 📊 Profit Margin %
* ⚠️ Late Delivery %

### Visuals

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Department
* Customer Segment Distribution
* Delivery Status Distribution
* Sales by City

## 📦 Page 2 – Product Performance Analysis

Analyzes product sales, category performance, pricing, and profitability.

### KPIs

* 🛒 Total Products
* 💰 Total Sales
* 📈 Total Profit
* 🏷️ Average Product Price

### Visuals

* Category Contribution (Treemap)
* Product Price vs Sales (Scatter Chart)
* Profitability Analysis
* Discount Impact on Sales
* Product Performance Matrix

## 👥 Page 3 – Customer & Geographic Analysis

Analyzes customer segments and geographic sales performance.

### KPIs

### Visuals
